About Best Liver and Onions Recipe – Classic, Easy & Full of Flavor

Liver and Onions is a traditional comfort food dish known for its rich flavor and nutritional benefits. Tender slices of liver are lightly coated and pan-fried, then paired with sweet, caramelized onions for a perfect balance of savory and sweet. This recipe is quick, simple, and packed with protein and essential nutrients like iron. It’s a classic homestyle meal that’s both hearty and satisfying, perfect for family dinners or those looking for a nutrient-dense dish.

How to Make

Step 1 Soak liver slices in milk for 20–30 minutes to reduce bitterness. Step 2 Remove liver from milk and pat dry. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder, then lightly coat with flour. Step 3 Heat butter or oil in a skillet over medium heat. Step 4 Add onions and cook until soft and caramelized. Remove and set aside. Step 5 In the same pan, cook liver slices for 2–3 minutes per side until browned but still tender inside. Step 6 Return onions to the pan and combine with liver. Step 7 Cook for another minute, then serve hot.

Pro Tips

✔ Don’t overcook the liver—it should stay tender, not tough. ✔ Soaking in milk helps reduce strong flavor and improves texture. ✔ Slice onions thinly for better caramelization. ✔ Use butter for richer flavor. ✔ Serve immediately for best taste.

FAQ

1. Why soak liver in milk? It helps reduce bitterness and improves flavor. 2. How do I know when liver is cooked? It should be browned outside and slightly pink inside. 3. Can I use chicken liver? Yes, but cooking time may vary. 4. Can I make it without flour? Yes, skip the coating for a lighter version. 5. What sides go well with it? Mashed potatoes, rice, or steamed vegetables.

Ingredients

1x
  • 1 lb beef liver sliced
  • 2 large onions thinly sliced
  • ½ cup milk (for soaking)
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp butter or olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt & pepper to taste
