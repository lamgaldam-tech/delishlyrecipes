About Best Low Carb Hamburger Meat Recipes – Easy, Healthy & High Protein Meals

Low Carb Hamburger Meat Recipes are easy, hearty, and perfect for anyone looking to enjoy delicious meals while reducing carbs. This simple skillet recipe combines seasoned ground beef with creamy sauce, cheese, and low-carb vegetables to create a satisfying and flavorful dish. It’s ideal for quick dinners, meal prep, or busy weeknights. With its rich taste and high-protein content, this recipe keeps you full and energized while supporting a low-carb lifestyle.

How to Make

Step 1 Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Step 2 Add chopped onion and garlic, sauté until soft. Step 3 Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat if needed. Step 4 Stir in paprika, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Step 5 Add cauliflower rice and cook for 3–5 minutes. Step 6 Pour in heavy cream and mix well. Step 7 Add shredded cheese and stir until melted and creamy. Step 8 Simmer for a few minutes, then serve hot.

Pro Tips

✔ Use full-fat ingredients for better flavor and keto benefits. ✔ Avoid overcooking cauliflower rice to keep a good texture. ✔ Add spinach, zucchini, or mushrooms for extra nutrients. ✔ Use fresh herbs for enhanced flavor. ✔ Adjust seasoning to your taste.

FAQ

1. Is hamburger meat good for low carb diets? Yes, it’s high in protein and naturally low in carbs. 2. Can I meal prep this recipe? Yes, store it in the fridge for up to 3–4 days. 3. Can I freeze it? Yes, freeze in airtight containers for up to 2 months. 4. What can I use instead of cauliflower rice? Zucchini noodles or simply serve it on its own. 5. How can I make it spicier? Add chili flakes or hot sauce.

Ingredients

1x
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 cup cauliflower rice
  • ½ cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 1 small onion chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt & pepper to taste
