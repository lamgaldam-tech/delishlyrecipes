Creamy Tuscan Garlic Chicken

Sun-dried tomatoes, garlic, and spinach in a creamy parmesan sauce over tender chicken breasts.

Prep

10 min

Cook

25 min

Servings

4

Calories

420

About Creamy Tuscan Garlic Chicken

Creamy Tuscan Garlic Chicken is a beloved dinner recipe that combines the robust flavors of Italy with a comforting creamy sauce. Tender chicken breasts are lightly seasoned and seared to a golden perfection. Heavy cream provides a luscious base, balanced by sharp parmesan cheese, creating a rich mouthfeel. Sun-dried tomatoes introduce a subtle tang, complementing the garlic's aromatic punch, while fresh spinach adds vibrancy and a hint of earthiness. This dish is visually stunning and full of flavor. It’s suitable for weekday meals or special dinners and pairs beautifully with pasta or crusty bread to soak up the sauce. The cooking process is simple but rewards careful attention, ensuring the chicken remains moist while the sauce is creamy and smooth. Variations with mushrooms, herbs, or different cheeses allow for personalization. Leftovers store well in the fridge and the sauce can be reheated gently. Overall, this recipe exemplifies Italian comfort food, combining practicality with indulgence, making it a go-to for home cooks seeking flavor, texture, and presentation in one dish.

How to Make

Season chicken with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat and sear chicken 5-6 minutes per side until golden, then set aside. Sauté minced garlic for 30 seconds until fragrant. Add sun-dried tomatoes and cook 2 minutes. Pour in heavy cream and stir in parmesan until melted. Add spinach and cook until wilted. Return chicken to skillet, spoon sauce over top, and simmer 5 minutes. Serve hot over pasta or with bread. Adjust seasoning to taste. Take care not to overcook chicken to retain moisture. Deglaze the pan with wine for added depth if desired. Cooking this dish develops layers of flavor, with each ingredient contributing texture and aroma. The sauce should be smooth and creamy, clinging to the chicken. Serve immediately to enjoy optimal taste, texture, and presentation. Garnish with fresh herbs for a final touch. This method ensures balanced flavors, combining richness, tang, and aromatics harmoniously.

Pro Tips

Use room temperature chicken for even cooking. Avoid overcooking to maintain juiciness. Sauté garlic just until fragrant to prevent bitterness. Deglaze pan with white wine for flavor depth. Add spinach at the end to maintain color and texture. Thin sauce with chicken stock if too thick. Customize with herbs like thyme or rosemary. Serve immediately; leftovers reheat gently. Heavy-bottom skillet helps distribute heat evenly. Ensure chicken is not overcrowded to allow searing. Adjust seasoning to taste before serving. This dish scales well, but cook in batches if needed. Use fresh ingredients for optimal flavor. Presentation is key: drizzle sauce over chicken and garnish with herbs or parmesan. Pair with sides to complete the meal. Enjoy the rich, creamy sauce and tender chicken in every bite. These tips guarantee consistent, delicious results.

Ingredients

  • 4 minced Garlic cloves
