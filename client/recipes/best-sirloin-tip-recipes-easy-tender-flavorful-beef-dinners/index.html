About Best Sirloin Tip Recipes – Easy, Tender & Flavorful Beef Dinners

Sirloin Tip Recipes are a great way to enjoy tender, flavorful beef in a simple and satisfying way. This version features sirloin tips simmered in a rich, savory gravy until perfectly tender. The result is a comforting, hearty dish that’s full of deep flavor. Perfect for family dinners or meal prep, this dish pairs beautifully with mashed potatoes, rice, or noodles. It’s easy to make, budget-friendly, and delivers restaurant-quality taste at home.

How to Make

Step 1 Heat olive oil in a large skillet or pot over medium heat. Step 2 Add sirloin tips and sear until browned on all sides. Remove and set aside. Step 3 In the same pan, sauté onion and garlic until soft and fragrant. Step 4 Return beef to the pan and add beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, and soy sauce. Step 5 Season with onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Step 6 Cover and simmer on low heat for 30–40 minutes until beef is tender. Step 7 Mix flour with a little water to form a slurry and stir into the gravy. Step 8 Cook until the gravy thickens. Step 9 Serve hot over mashed potatoes, rice, or noodles.

Pro Tips

✔ Use low heat and slow cooking for the most tender beef. ✔ Sear the meat well to enhance flavor. ✔ Add mushrooms for extra richness. ✔ Adjust gravy thickness by controlling the slurry amount. ✔ Let the dish rest a few minutes before serving for better flavor.

FAQ

1. What is the best way to cook sirloin tips? Slow simmering or slow cooking helps make them tender and flavorful. 2. Can I use a slow cooker? Yes, cook on low for 6–8 hours or high for 3–4 hours. 3. Can I freeze this recipe? Yes, store in an airtight container for up to 2 months. 4. Why are my sirloin tips tough? They may need more cooking time to become tender. 5. What sides go best with sirloin tips? Mashed potatoes, rice, egg noodles, or steamed vegetables.

Ingredients

1x
  • 1½ lbs sirloin tip steak cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt & pepper to taste
