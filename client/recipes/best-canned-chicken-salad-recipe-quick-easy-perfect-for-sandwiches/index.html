About Best Canned Chicken Salad Recipe – Quick, Easy & Perfect for Sandwiches

Canned Chicken Salad is a quick, easy, and budget-friendly meal that comes together in just minutes. Using canned chicken saves time while still providing a high-protein, flavorful dish. The creamy dressing combined with crisp vegetables creates a satisfying texture perfect for sandwiches, wraps, lettuce cups, or as a standalone salad. This recipe is ideal for busy weekdays, meal prep, or casual gatherings. It’s versatile, flavorful, and can be customized with your favorite add-ins like nuts, fruits, or fresh herbs.

How to Make

Step 1 Place the drained canned chicken in a mixing bowl. Step 2 Add chopped celery, red onion, and optional grapes or apples. Step 3 In a small bowl, mix mayonnaise, lemon juice, salt, and black pepper. Step 4 Pour the dressing over the chicken mixture. Step 5 Gently stir until all ingredients are evenly coated. Step 6 Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. Step 7 Optional: Chill in the refrigerator for 20–30 minutes before serving. Step 8 Serve in sandwiches, wraps, lettuce cups, or with crackers.

Pro Tips

✔ Use white canned chicken for a mild flavor. ✔ Chill before serving for the best taste. ✔ Add toasted nuts like almonds or walnuts for extra crunch. ✔ Replace mayonnaise with Greek yogurt for a lighter version. ✔ Customize with diced apples, grapes, or dried cranberries for a touch of sweetness.

FAQ

1. Can I make this salad ahead of time? Yes, it can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 3 days in an airtight container. 2. Can I freeze canned chicken salad? Freezing is not recommended, as the mayonnaise can separate when thawed. 3. What can I serve with this salad? Sandwich bread, wraps, crackers, or fresh greens work great. 4. Can I make it healthier? Yes, substitute Greek yogurt for mayonnaise or reduce the amount of dressing. 5. Can I add vegetables or fruits? Absolutely! Try adding diced apples, grapes, cucumbers, or bell peppers.

Ingredients

1x
  • 2 cans (12.5 oz / 354 g each) canned chicken drained
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 celery stalk finely chopped
  • ¼ cup red onion finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley chopped (optional)
  • ¼ cup chopped grapes or apples (optional for sweetness)
