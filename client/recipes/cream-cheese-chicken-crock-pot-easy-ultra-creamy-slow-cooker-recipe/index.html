Cream Cheese Chicken Crock Pot – Easy & Ultra Creamy Slow Cooker Recipe

Cream Cheese Chicken Crock Pot is the ultimate creamy comfort food made with simple ingredients and minimal prep time. This easy slow cooker recipe combines tender, juicy chicken with rich cream cheese and savory seasonings to create a smooth, flavorful sauce that melts in your mouth. Just toss everything into the crock pot and let it cook to perfection — no complicated steps required!

Prep

10 min

Cook

360 min

Servings

6

Calories

480

About Cream Cheese Chicken Crock Pot – Easy & Ultra Creamy Slow Cooker Recipe

Cream Cheese Chicken Crock Pot is a rich, creamy, and comforting slow cooker dinner that requires minimal effort but delivers maximum flavor. The chicken becomes incredibly tender as it slowly cooks in a velvety cream cheese sauce infused with ranch seasoning and garlic. This is a perfect dump-and-go recipe for busy weeknights, meal prep, or cozy family dinners. Serve it over rice, pasta, mashed potatoes, or even cauliflower rice for a low-carb option.

How to Make

Step 1: Place the chicken breasts at the bottom of your crock pot. Step 2: Add minced garlic, black pepper, paprika, and ranch seasoning over the chicken. Step 3: Pour chicken broth around the chicken. Step 4: Place the cream cheese on top (do not mix yet). Step 5: Cover and cook: LOW: 6 hours HIGH: 4 hours Step 6: Once cooked, shred the chicken using two forks and stir everything together until the sauce becomes smooth and creamy. Step 7: Garnish with parsley and serve warm.

Pro Tips

✔ Use block cream cheese, not spreadable tub cream cheese. ✔ For extra flavor, add ½ cup shredded cheddar at the end. ✔ Want it thicker? Let it cook uncovered for 15 minutes after shredding. ✔ Add mushrooms or spinach for extra nutrition. ✔ For spicy version, add red pepper flakes or diced jalapeños.

FAQ

1. Can I use frozen chicken? Yes, but increase cooking time by 1 hour and ensure it reaches 165°F (75°C). 2. Can I make this low carb? Yes! Serve it with cauliflower rice or zucchini noodles. 3. How do I store leftovers? Store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 4 days. 4. Can I freeze it? Yes, freeze for up to 2 months. Reheat gently to maintain creamy texture. 5. What can I serve it with? Rice, pasta, mashed potatoes, roasted vegetables, or dinner rolls.

Ingredients

1x
  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 8 oz (225g) cream cheese softened
  • 1 packet ranch seasoning mix
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon paprika (optional)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)
