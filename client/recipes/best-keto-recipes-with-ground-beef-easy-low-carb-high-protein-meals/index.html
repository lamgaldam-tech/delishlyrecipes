About Best Keto Recipes with Ground Beef – Easy, Low Carb & High Protein Meals

Keto Recipes with Ground Beef are simple, hearty, and packed with flavor while keeping carbs low. This easy skillet version combines seasoned ground beef with creamy sauce, cheese, and cauliflower rice to create a satisfying and nutritious meal. It’s perfect for busy weeknights or meal prep, offering a high-protein, low-carb option that doesn’t sacrifice taste. The creamy texture and rich flavors make it a comforting dish that fits perfectly into a keto lifestyle.

How to Make

Step 1 Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Step 2 Add chopped onion and garlic, sauté until soft. Step 3 Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat if needed. Step 4 Stir in paprika, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Step 5 Add cauliflower rice and cook for 3–5 minutes. Step 6 Pour in heavy cream and stir well. Step 7 Add shredded cheese and mix until melted and creamy. Step 8 Simmer for a few minutes, then serve hot.

Pro Tips

✔ Use full-fat ingredients for best keto results. ✔ Don’t overcook cauliflower rice to keep texture light. ✔ Add spinach or mushrooms for extra nutrients. ✔ Use ground turkey as a lean alternative if desired. ✔ Adjust seasoning to taste for more flavor.

FAQ

1. Is ground beef good for keto? Yes, it’s high in protein and fats, making it perfect for keto diets. 2. Can I meal prep this recipe? Yes, store in the fridge for up to 3–4 days. 3. Can I freeze it? Yes, freeze in airtight containers for up to 2 months. 4. What can I use instead of cauliflower rice? You can use zucchini noodles or serve it alone. 5. How do I make it spicier? Add chili flakes or hot sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
